GENERATIVE AI FUNDAMENTALS

They appreciated the ethical issues and implications of generative AI. They were introduced to the potential career opportunities in generative AI. Learners were privy to the insights from AI experts for being successful with generative AI. By completing the hands-on labs and projects, the learners explored the use cases of generative AI through tools like OpenAI ChatGPT.
