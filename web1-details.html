BARANGAY WEBSITE

This project is a fully functional website designed to serve as a digital platform for barangay officials and residents. Its primary purpose is to improve communication, transparency, and accessibility of services within the community. The website allows barangay officials to efficiently disseminate important information, such as community announcements, government advisories, emergency alerts, and updates on local programs or events.

Beyond broadcasting news and announcements, the website also acts as an interactive service portal. It includes structured forms and contact information to help guide users through common government transactions.

It reflects practical web development skills while also highlighting a commitment to using technology for public good.
